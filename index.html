About us

O nama

We provide worldwide moving services with a professional team that has more than 20 years of experience in the moving industry. Our office is located in Belgrade, but we operate throughout Serbia and Montenegro.

Sa timom koji ima više od 20 godina iskustva pružamo usluge selidbe za klijente iz svih krajeva sveta. Naša kancelarija se nalazi u Beogradu, ali poslujemo širom Srbije i Crne Gore.

We have the right tools and the right attitude to get the job done. Your move will be customized to your needs with the assistance of your personal moving consultant, professional movers, drivers, and equipment.

Bilo da se doseljavate iz inostranstva, samo menjate deo grada u kome živite ili želite da preselite kompaniju iz jednog mesta u drugo, odlučite se za Intermove. Mi imamo znanje koje će vam pružiti besprekornu uslugu selidbe. Sve vrste selidbi prilagođavaju se vašim potre­bama uz pomoć ličnog konsultanta, profesionalnog tima za pakovanje i upravljanje selid­bom, iskusnih vozača i savreme­ne opreme. U toku selidbe možete se u potpunosti opustiti, jer su vaše stvari kod nas na sigurnom.

Relocation process

Simple professional approach. Your moving consultant will support and guide you throughout the entire relocation process, starting from the moment you decide to move until you reach your desired destination.

Proces selidbe

Jednostavan i profesionalni pristup. Vaš lični konsultant za selidbu pružiće vam podršku i vodiće vas kroz ceo proces preseljenja, počevši od trenutka kada se odlučite na selidbu do trenutka kada dospete na željenu lokaciju.

1

Get in touch

Kontaktirajte nas

Call us and discuss your plans with the relocation consultant. We will listen carefully and guide you through the basic steps and information depending on the nature of your move type.

Pozovite nas i razgovarajte o svojim planovima sa konsul­tantom za selidbu. Saslušaćemo vas pažljivo i sprovesti kroz osnovne korake i informacije u zavisnosti od vrste vaše selidbe.

2

We see & listen

Gledamo i slušamo

Our moving consultant will make a free survey. During the survey, we will make an inventory of what needs to be moved so that we can provide a smooth transition to your new home. The moving consultant will take all the details carefully and with full attention so we can provide you with an estimate for your budget and needs.

Naš konsultant za selidbe uradiće besplatnu procenu. Tokom procene ćemo popisati šta sve treba preseliti, kako bismo osigurali prijatno iskustvo pre­laska u novi dom. Konsultant za selidbe će pažljivo i posvećeno uzeti u obzir sve detalje tako da možemo da vam damo tačnu procenu troškova i vaših potreba.

3

Let’s move

Selimo se

Our team comes to you and begins with packaging. They will use the highest quality materials and specially made boxes for sensitive items. A team of packing experts will ensure that your belongings are safely packed, loaded and transported.

Naš tim dolazi do vas i počinje sa pakovanjem. Koristiće najkva­litetnije materijale i posebno napravljenje sanduke za osetljive predmete. Tim eksperata za pakovanje pobrinuće se da se vaše stvari bezbedno spakuju, prenesu i utovare.

4

Settling in

Dobro došli u novi dom

Together with you, we will travel across the globe or just next door and we will meet at your new home. Our professional team will accommodate your needs and agreed services, they will settle your valuables and your furniture at your new home at your destination, and remove the waste so that you can start your journey and move forward enjoying your new home.

Bilo da sa vama prolazimo celu planetu ili vas selimo samo u susedni stan, zajedno ulazimo u vaš novi dom. Naš profesionalni tim će izaći u susret vašim potrebama i dogovorenim uslo­vima, postaviće dragocenosti i nameštaj onako kako vi to želite i pobrinuće se da ostavimo vaš dom čistim kako biste mogli nesmetano i bez odlaganja da uživate u novom okruženju.
